Kick Back and Enjoy the Game

The 25 plus tv's coordinated with a custom sound system make the Landshark Bar &Grill the premier place to watch and listen to the sounds of every MSU game

Are you looking for a fun place to hang out on the weekend? Pick a local bar that has great food, drinks and service. Landshark Bar &Grill has been a preferred local sports bar in East Lansing, MI for over 30 years. Many alumni consider us to be an essential part of the Michigan State University experience.


Come to our bar and grill today for excellent service and a fun, lively and welcoming environment.

Landsharks Allstar lineup:

Delicious Drinks: Our modern twist on the famous shark bowl, coming in three different flavors! Specialty cocktails for any time of the day!

We offer outstanding customer service

We're more than your standard college bar. When you visit our local sports bar, you can expect prompt and friendly service from our staff. You'll enjoy our nightly drink specials. Our bartenders and servers are always happy to give you food and drink recommendations. We also have over two dozen TVs, making our restaurant the best place to catch a big game.
